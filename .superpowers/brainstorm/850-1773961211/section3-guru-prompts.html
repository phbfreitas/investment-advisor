3B. What Each Prompt Does

💡 Investment Suggestions

Pre-fills: "Based on my investment strategy and your core philosophy, suggest specific tickers to buy or sell. Consider my current holdings, sector targets, and risk tolerance."

📊 Comprehensive Financial Analysis

Pre-fills: "Analyze my entire financial situation — investments, net worth, and cash flow (budget vs. actual). Identify strengths, risks, and suggest adjustments."

⚖️ Portfolio Rebalancing

Pre-fills: "Review my portfolio against my target allocations and suggest specific buy/sell orders to rebalance. Prioritize actions by impact and alignment with my strategy."

🏥 Full Financial Health Audit

Pre-fills: "Perform a complete financial health audit — assets beyond investments, total net worth, cash flow (budgeted vs. actual), debt ratios, and emergency fund adequacy. Recommend changes."

3C. Behavior

  • Clicking a prompt pill fills the chat input with the pre-crafted text — user can edit before sending
  • Prompts replace the existing quick prompts on the empty-state welcome screen
  • Prompts are defined in a PROMPT_TEMPLATES constant array — easy to add more later
  • Each prompt works with whichever personas are selected — no guru-specific variants needed
  • The advisor's system prompt + RAG context + user profile already provide all the personalization
